# Client Portal User Guide

Welcome to the Client Portal. This secure platform allows you to access your financial documents shared by your Chartered Accountant (CA).

## 1. Accessing the Portal

1.  Open the link provided by your CA (e.g., `http://localhost:5174/portal`).
2.  **Login**:
    - **Phone Number**: Enter your registered mobile number.
    - **Password**: Enter the password provided by your CA.
3.  Click **"Sign In"**.

## 2. Dashboard

Upon logging in, you will see your **Dashboard**.
- **Recent Documents**: Quick access to lately uploaded files.
- **Messages**: Recent communications from your CA.

## 3. My Documents

### Viewing Documents
1.  Click on **"My Documents"**.
2.  Documents are organized by **Year** (e.g., 2023-24, 2024-25).
3.  Click on a year to expand it.

### Downloading Files
1.  Locate the document you need (e.g., "ITR 2024").
2.  Click the **Download** icon.
3.  The file will be securely downloaded to your device.

### Filtering
- Use the **Search** bar to find specific files.
- Filter by **Tag** (e.g., "Show only GST Returns").

## 4. Messages

- Check the **Messages** tab for updates from your CA.
- You can view shared files sent via messages here.

## 5. Security Tips

- **Logout**: Always click "Sign Out" when you are finished, especially on public computers.
- **Password**: Request a password change if you suspect unauthorized access.
